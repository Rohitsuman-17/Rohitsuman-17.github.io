About Me
My Story

I'm Rohit Suman, an MBA candidate specializing in Business Analytics at Symbiosis Institute of Business Management, Bengaluru. My journey into analytics began with a curiosity for understanding how data shapes decisions — from marketing campaigns to financial portfolios to supply chains.

Growing up in Jaipur, I pursued a B.Sc. in Science before transitioning into business — where I discovered my passion for bridging quantitative thinking with strategic insight. That intersection — where data meets decision-making — is where I thrive.

I've applied this thinking across real-world projects: analyzing football performance with Tableau, building marketing dashboards in Power BI, winning the IIT Madras Dataverse 2026 case competition, and delivering measurable results during my e-commerce internship.

My goal is to become a versatile analytics professional who can operate across domains — not siloed in one function, but capable of driving impact across the entire business landscape.
